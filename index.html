Alien: Earth

A sci‑fi series that brings an alien threat to our home planet for the first time.

About the Series

Alien: Earth is a science fiction horror television series created by Noah Hawley, based on the Alien universe. Set on Earth in the near future, it follows a group of scientists, soldiers, and corporate agents as they confront a rapidly evolving alien threat.

The show combines deep character drama with suspense, body horror, and big questions about corporate power, AI, and survival.

  • The Setup: The horror begins when a Weyland‑Yutani research ship, the USCSS Maginot, crashes into a city owned by their rival, the Prodigy Corporation, unleashing Xenomorphs onto the planet.
  • The Protagonists: The central characters are a group of young "Hybrids"—dying human minds transferred into synthetic bodies by Prodigy's CEO, Boy Kavalier. The main Hybrid, Wendy, has a unique ability to sense or potentially communicate with the Xenomorphs.
  • Key Themes: The series explores the dangers of corporate greed, ethical issues of AI/experimentation, and the struggle for humanity and survival.
  • The Threat: In addition to classic Xenomorphs, the show introduces new alien lifeforms, setting the stage for intense action and body horror.
